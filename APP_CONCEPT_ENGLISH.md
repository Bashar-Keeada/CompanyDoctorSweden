## ðŸ’¡ The Concept

### Core Idea
Company Doktor transforms traditional business consulting into an accessible, systematic process using medical terminology:

- **Check-Up** = Initial business analysis
- **Diagnosis** = Deep-dive problem identification
- **Prescription** = Actionable business plan
- **Treatment** = Implementation and coaching
- **Follow-Up** = Continuous monitoring and optimization

### Why Medical Metaphors Work
1. **Universal Understanding** - Everyone knows the doctor-patient relationship
2. **Reduces Anxiety** - Makes business consulting less intimidating
3. **Systematic Approach** - Clear, structured process
4. **Ongoing Care** - Emphasizes continuous improvement
5. **Trust Building** - Medical professionals are highly trusted
